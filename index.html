PROJECT 1.

WHAT WILL HAPPEN AFTER SPLASH

What happens after Hockney's splash?
Fashion can spend everyday life with human movement if a painting can express the same as a dot of the moment.
In that respect, I was inspired by Hockney's splashing water, and I would like to talk about fashion after that.

PROJECT 2.

MEDIVAL VILLAINS

The question arises as to why villains are expressed darkly.
Knowing that the etymology of villains was peasantry, the word villain was added to the word evil after their urbanization life.
At that time, the adaptation of peasants was linked to medieval peddlers to express their adaptation to urbanization.
